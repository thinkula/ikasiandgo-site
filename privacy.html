Ikasi & Go

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Ikasi & Go is a Basque vocabulary learning app. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have.

What data we collect

Ikasi & Go stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your quiz progress and SRS (spaced repetition) scores
  • Your streak and session history
  • Your app settings and preferences
  • Your subscription or trial status

None of this data is transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties. It lives entirely on your device and is deleted if you uninstall the app or use the Reset button in the Browse screen.

Vocabulary content

The app fetches vocabulary data from ikasiandgo.com/vocabulary.json when you open it. This request does not include any personal information — it is a simple public file download, the same as loading any webpage.

Purchases and subscriptions

All payments are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment details, card numbers, or billing information. Apple's privacy policy governs all transactions.

Analytics and tracking

We do not use analytics tools, advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, or any third-party data collection services. We do not track your behaviour, location, or device identifiers.

Children's privacy

Ikasi & Go does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app contains no user accounts, no messaging, and no data collection that would implicate children's privacy regulations.

Data retention and deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are in full control. To delete all your data, use the Reset progress button in the Browse screen, or uninstall the app. We have no server-side data to delete on your behalf.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy we will update the date at the top and, where appropriate, notify you through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy please contact us at hello@ikasiandgo.com.
